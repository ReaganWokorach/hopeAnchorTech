Computer Fundamentals

What You Will Learn
Computer hardware: parts and functions

Basic digital safety and online etiquette

Cybersecurity Awareness

2 Weeks Beginner Online & In-Person

In an increasingly connected world, everyone needs to understand basic digital safety. This short, meaningful course teaches you how to protect yourself, your family, and your organisation from online threats, explained simply and practically. Available free of charge to all.

What You Will Learn
What cybersecurity is and why it matters
Recognising and avoiding phishing scams
Strong passwords and two-factor authentication
Safe use of public Wi-Fi and mobile data
Social engineering and impersonation scams
Protecting personal data and financial accounts
Mobile phone security best practices
What to do if you are hacked or scammed
Who is this for? Everyone, students, seniors, entrepreneurs, NGO staff, and anyone using the internet or a smartphone, anywhere in the world. Enrol for Free

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to join?

Absolutely not. Our Computer Fundamentals and Cybersecurity courses are designed for complete beginners, no prior experience of any kind is required.

Can I join online from outside Uganda?

Yes! All our training courses are available online via Zoom or Google Meet. You can join from anywhere in the world. Africa, Europe, the diaspora, or beyond.

How are the free courses funded?

Our social enterprise model means that revenue from our paid business and technical services directly subsidises free training for vulnerable community members.

Do I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every student who successfully completes a course receives a Hope Anchor Tech Certificate of Completion, which you can add to your CV or LinkedIn profile.

Can organisations or schools book group training?

Absolutely. We offer group and corporate training packages, in-person or online — tailored to your organisation's needs. Contact us for a custom quote.

When do new cohorts start?

We run rolling cohorts throughout the year. Contact us to find out the next available start date for the course you are interested in.

Ready to start learning?

Send us a message today, in Gulu or from anywhere in the world — and we'll get you enrolled in the next available cohort.
