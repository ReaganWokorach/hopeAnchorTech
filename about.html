About HopeAnchor Tech

A faith-driven social enterprise born in the heart of Gulu — anchored in purpose, reaching the world.

How It Started

Born from a Verse,
Built for the World

HopeAnchor Tech was founded on the conviction that technology is not a privilege — it is a tool of liberation. Inspired by James 2:17 — "Faith without works is dead" — our founder Reagan Wokorach set out to demonstrate that faith without action changes nothing, but faith backed by skill and service can transform lives.

Based in Gulu Municipality, Northern Uganda, HopeAnchor Tech was established to address the widening digital divide in the region. We saw young people, single parents, job-seekers, and community members locked out of economic opportunity simply because they lacked access to digital skills and services. We decided to do something about it.

We operate as a social enterprise: our corporate and professional service fees subsidise entirely free foundational training for vulnerable community members. No one is turned away because they cannot pay. And while our roots are in Gulu, our services are available to anyone anywhere in the world — online.

"Our anchor is Christ. Our works are tech. Our mission is people."
Reagan Wokorach, Founder & CEO
What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities in Northern Uganda and around the world through accessible, high-quality technology education and professional services, anchored in Christian values and driven by measurable social impact.

Our Vision

A world where every person, regardless of background, geography, or economic status, possesses the digital skills to participate confidently in the modern economy and fulfil their God-given potential.

Founder & CEO
Meet the Founder

Reagan Wokorach

Reagan Wokorach is a technology professional and social entrepreneur with over 5 years of hands-on experience in institutional data labeling, having worked with global AI and machine learning firms including Samasource Uganda. He combines deep technical expertise with a profound faith commitment and a burning passion for community development in Northern Uganda.

His experience at Samasource Uganda's Gulu Branch, one of East Africa's pioneering AI delivery centres gave him first-hand knowledge of how technology can create dignified employment in post-conflict communities. This experience directly shaped the vision for HopeAnchor Tech.

Reagan holds a Diploma in Information Technology from Cavendish University Uganda and is an active member of the Christian community in Gulu. He believes that the greatest technology transfer is not hardware or software, it is skill, confidence, and opportunity given to a person who was told they had none.

Our Social Model

How We Sustain Free Training

Our hybrid revenue model ensures sustainability without compromising on our social mission.

01

Corporate & Professional Services

Web development, IT consulting, data labeling, and advanced training tracks generate revenue at fair market rates, locally and globally.

02

Revenue Reinvested

Profits are channelled directly into subsidising operations, facilities, and instructor time for free community programmes.

03

Free Community Training

Vulnerable community members, youth, and anyone who cannot afford training receive quality education at zero cost.
